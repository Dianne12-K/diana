Geomatics Engineering

Geomatics engineering is a field that focuses on spatial information. It involves the collection, analysis, interpretation, and presentation of data related to the earth's surface and its features.

Key principles in geomatics include geographic information systems (GIS), remote sensing, surveying, cartography, and geodesy. Geomatics engineering plays a crucial role in various industries such as urban planning, environmental management, natural resource exploration, transportation, and disaster management.
